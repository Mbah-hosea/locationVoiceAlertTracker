About Transport Tracker

Transport Tracker is a web platform designed to help users monitor transport routes, receive alerts, and stay aware of their location. It is user-friendly, multilingual, and future-ready for mobile integration and backend enhancements.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Alerts: Get notifications for stops or general location awareness.
  • Custom Messages: Users can create their own alert messages with TTS support.
  • Simple Inputs: Only city and spot needed; no latitude or longitude required.
  • Responsive Design: Works on desktops and mobile devices with a clean interface.
  • Future Backend Integration: Supports database storage and voice uploads.
